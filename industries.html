ECO-NINJA'S

Be the change you want to see in the world: Reduce, Reuse, Recycle.

  • ZERO WASTE MANAGEMENT(INDUSTRIES)
    • Practice e-waste management.
    • Appoint a waste prevention team to brainstorm ideas, raise awareness and motivate staff.
    • Reuse incoming packaging such as boxes for outgoing orders and envelopes for sending internal mail.
    • Use refillable printer cartridges and rechargeable batteries.
    • Reuse what you consume (and cannot refuse or reduce)
    • Recycle mobile phones, toner and printer cartridges and light bulbs for a charity
    • Repurpose old things like using old tee shirts as cleaning rags or food containers as storage containers for other things like office supplies.
