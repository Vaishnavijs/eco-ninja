    ECO FLOWER POT

  • MATERIALS NEEDED
  • Potting soil
  • Plants or herbs
  • Paint brushes
  • coconuts
  • PROCEDURE
  • Coconuts should be cleaned both inside and out.
  • After that, paint an emoji of your choosing on top of the coconuts.
  • Allow for thorough drying of the paint.
  • Fill the coconut halfway with fresh soil and insert the plant you wish to grow.
  • More plotting soil should be applied to the rest of the coconut.
  • After you've planted the plant, give it some water.
  • Your coconut planter is now complete.

  • Used Plastic Bottles
  • Leftover plastic waste
  • PROCEDURE
  • The plastic bottles should be cleaned both inside and out.

  • Fill the bottle with the plastic packets, make sure that all the space in the bottle is covered.

  • Collect them and make sure you make them everytime you notice that a lot of plastuc has been consumed at your place
